Moab

Moab is a really fun place to go for vacation! Especially in early spring or the summer, there are a lot of amazing things you can go to and see there! Theres also many fun activies you can do in the area while you visit!

Here are some places I would reccomend going to in Moab that makes it worth going that other people love to go to!

  • Arches National Park
  • Dead Horse Point State Park
  • Moab Giants
  • Western River Expeditions

Arches National Park

Arches National Park has over 2000 stone arches! They also have unique rock-formations that people like to go see. Its a perfect place for photography of the scenery that is offered there.
They also have activities like hiking, camping, and stargazing that you can do there aswell!

Dead Horse Point State Park

Dead Horse Point has lanscapes of canyons and an overlook to a gooseneck bend. When your looking over this bend, visitors can see the colorado river curving through the canyons, giving off a pretty view.
The park also has trails were people can go mountain biking and hiking that surrounds the river.

Moab Giants

The Moab Giants is an indoor and outdoor dinosaur museum. There are fun trails you can do there, a 5D aquarium and a 3D theater. Theres also a cafe and gift shop you can visit!

Western River Expeditions

At the Western River Expeditions in Moab you can go rafting through the Colorado River,the most known one.
There are also other tours that they offer like Cataract Canyon, Desolation Canyon, and theres other jetboat doors aswell.
