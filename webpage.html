About hiking

What to bring

  1. Water
  2. Food
  3. First aid kit
  4. Sunscreen

You should bring water and food to keep you energized and hydrated. The first aid kit is good to have incase you fall or get hurt while hiking and need to take care of it.

What you should wear

What to wear when your going hiking

  1. Hiking boots or shoes
  2. Quick drying pants
  3. Hat

These things should ensure a good and smooth walk while your going hiking, the hat is to protect your face from the sun
